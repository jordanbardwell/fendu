# Fendu — Feature Ideas

## Widgets & Live Activities
- **Lock Screen widget** — Remaining balance for the current paycheck
- **Home Screen widget** — Mini breakdown bar (allocated vs. remaining)
- **Live Activity** — During paycheck week, show countdown to payday with current allocation status

## Insights & Trends (Pro)
- **"Where did it go?" monthly summary** — Pie/bar chart of spending by account category
- **Paycheck-over-paycheck comparison** — "You spent $200 more this paycheck vs. last"
- **Savings velocity** — Track how fast savings accounts grow across paychecks

## Smart Notifications
- **Bill reminders** — "Rent ($1,200) is coming out of your next paycheck"
- **Overspending alerts** — "You've allocated 90% of this paycheck with 4 days left"
- **Payday notification** — "Paycheck arrived! You have $X to allocate"

## Goals / Savings Targets
- Attach a **goal amount + target date** to any savings account
- **Progress ring** on the account card with percentage
- Calculate "you need to save $X per paycheck to hit this goal"

## Shared Budgets (Pro)
- **Partner/roommate mode** via CloudKit sharing
- Each person sees the same paycheck splits and can add their own allocations

## Themes & Customization
- **App icon picker** — 5-6 alternate icons (dark, minimal, gradient, etc.)
- **Accent color picker** — Swap brand green for a preferred color

## Shortcuts & Siri Integration
- "Hey Siri, how much do I have left this paycheck?"
- **App Intents** for adding a quick transaction without opening the app

## Export & Reports
- **PDF/CSV export** of paycheck history
- Useful for tax prep, financial planning, sharing with a partner/advisor
